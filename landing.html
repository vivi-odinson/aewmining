About ME

The story I own, that not many know.

MY History

It all started in the year 1978 when Mr. Amit Kumar Goswami inherited this fine trait of entrepreneurial skill from his father, Sri. Anil Kanta Goswami, who was a sole agent for IISCO to market and sell their CI-SPUN Pipe for PAN India. That's how Sri. Amit ji set up a trading firm in my name, Amit Engineering Works, to deal with various equipage related to water supply and sewearge projects. Cast Iron, Double Flange Pipes, Fittings and Valves, were all he dealt with. He procured finest quality products from the casting hub of Howra, Kolkata and was supplying to various Government contractors to meet their demands.

The sudden demise of Amit ji compelled his son, Mr. Avik Kumar Goswami, to take over the firm at the budding age of 17. Giving substance to the same, Avik came to quickly grasp the business running order.

The Expansion

He expanded the DI-PIPE business vertical of different water-boards to various states in India. Partnering with renowned manufacturers like Electro Steel Casting Ltd., Jai Balaji Industries Ltd., Tata Metalliks Ltd., Reshmi Metalliks Ltd., etc. to meet the demand.

Rhoncus magna

Eventually, he ventured into a very succesful mining service, adding another wing to his business.

The vertical of raising contract of Iron ore mining commenced in April 2017. It's been witnessing the exponential growth of this sector, exceeding the expectations of committed customers, continuing quality services.
